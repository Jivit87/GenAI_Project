# Seattle & King County Real Estate Regulations

1. **Short-Term Rental Restrictions**: Most condos in Seattle buildings restricted short-term rentals (Airbnb/VRBO) through their HOA bylaws. Single-family homes are generally permitted but require a Short-Term Rental Operator's License.

2. **Fair Housing & Tenant Laws**: King County has some of the strictest tenant protection laws in the country. This includes the "First-in-Time" ordinance and limits on security deposits.

3. **Climate & Environmental Disclosures**: Sellers must disclose information about flood zones, landslide risks (common in hilly Seattle areas), and seismic activity potential.

4. **ADU/DADU Zoning**: The "Housing Choices" legislation has legalized detached accessory dwelling units (DADUs) in many residential zones, allowing for higher density and investment potential.
