RERA (Real Estate Regulation Act) mandates developer registration, protecting buyers from project delays.
Stamp duty is a mandatory state tax and typically ranges from 4% to 8% of the total property value.
Capital gains tax: Short-term (owned < 2 years) is taxed at the income slab rate; Long-term is taxed at 20% with indexation benefits.
Foreign investment in real estate is tightly governed by FEMA (Foreign Exchange Management Act) regulations.
Rental income must be declared under 'Income from House Property' for tax purposes if it exceeds standard deduction limits.
