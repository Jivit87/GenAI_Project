# Real Estate Investment Tips: King County

1. **Focus on Cash Flow over Appreciation**: In a high-interest rate environment, long-term investors should prioritize properties where rental income covers the mortgage and maintenance. Do not rely solely on speculative price appreciation.

2. **The "Tech Corridor" Advantage**: Investing in properties within a 15-minute commute of major tech hubs (Amazon HQ, Microsoft, Google) provides a "safety net" for occupancy and resale value.

3. **Light Rail Proximity**: Properties within walking distance (0.5 - 1 mile) of current or planned Light Rail stations historically outperform the broader market in Appreciation.

4. **Condition Assessment**: The King County market is sensitive to the "Condition of the House". Properties in 'Good' or 'Excellent' condition command a significant premium and sell 30% faster than those in 'Fair' condition.

5. **Multi-Family & ADUs**: New zoning laws in many King County municipalities have made it easier to add Accessory Dwelling Units (ADUs). This is an excellent way to boost ROI on single-family properties.
