# Seattle & King County Market Trends (2024-2025)

The Seattle and King County real estate market has transitioned into a more balanced environment in 2025. Key observations include:

1. **The $675k Threshold**: There is a significant divergence in the market around the $675,000 price point. Properties below this threshold, such as older condos and townhomes in peripheral areas, have seen softer demand and longer days on market. Conversely, properties above $675k in desirable, tech-heavy zones (Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland) remain highly competitive.

2. **Inventory Growth**: 2025 has seen a substantial increase in available inventory compared to previous years, giving buyers more leverage and reducing the intensity of bidding wars in most segments.

3. **Interest Rate Impact**: Elevated mortgage rates hovering around 6-7% have kept affordability a challenge, but strong employment in the tech sector maintains a steady floor for demand in premium neighborhoods.

4. **Transit Drivers**: Proximity to the Sound Transit Light Rail expansion remains one of the strongest predictors of property value retention and growth. Areas like Northgate, Lynnwood, and the Eastside Corridor are seeing sustained interest.
